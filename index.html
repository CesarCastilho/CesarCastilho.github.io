I work with mathematical epidemiology, nonlinear dynamics and applications of mathematics to complex biological systems.

About me

I am César Castilho, Associate Professor at the Department of Mathematics, Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE), Brazil. My research interests include mathematical epidemiology, dynamical systems, point vortex dynamics, population dynamics, and mathematical modeling of biological and ecological systems.

I am also involved in undergraduate and graduate education in mathematics, with particular interest in mathematical modeling, differential equations, computational methods, and interdisciplinary applications.

Research

My current research focuses on mathematical models for infectious disease dynamics, arbovirus persistence, spatial heterogeneity, urban networks, and nonlinear systems arising in biology and fluid dynamics.
